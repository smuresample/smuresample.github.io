Welcome

Welcome to The Phoenix Children’s Center. We are a pediatric practice in Phoenix, Arizona established in 1971 by our friend and colleague, Dr. Neal Berger. Our office is staffed by 4 Pediatric Providers. We have both male and female physicians.

We provide medical care for children from birth through 21 years of age. We are open 5 days a week year round excluding major holidays. We are able to assist you with your after hour emergencies by phone. We know parenting is a 24/7 job and we want help to be available to you 24/7.

Come visit our new office at The Camelback Arboleda at 16th Street and Highland. Our experienced and friendly staff is waiting to assist you. Call for an appointment today.
